LoRaWAN Network Partner

LoRaWAN Network Partner

Netmore Group

Meshteq is partnering with Netmore to provide carrier-grade LoRaWAN Network-as-a-Service (NaaS) in Malaysia and selected regional markets.

This collaboration combines:

  • Meshteq’s solution design, sensor and gateway deployment, and analytics platform
  • Netmore’s managed LoRaWAN core network and global roaming footprint

Together, we enable utilities, cities and industrial operators to deploy secure, scalable IoT networks without the burden of building and operating their own infrastructure.

What This Enables

  • Nationwide / regional LoRaWAN coverage with predictable SLAs
  • Secure, managed connectivity for critical assets and infrastructure
  • Flexible commercial models: NaaS, per-device or project-based deployments

To discuss LoRaWAN projects with Meshteq and Netmore, email business@meshteq.com.
