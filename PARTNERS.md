# Meshteq Partners & Collaborations

Meshteq collaborates with selected organisations to deliver IoT, LoRaWAN and AI-based
solutions across energy, utilities, smart cities and industrial sectors.

This document outlines our current strategic partnerships and the types of collaborations
we are interested in.

---

## 1. Strategic Network Partner

### Netmore (LoRaWAN Network Partner)

- **Role:** Provides managed LoRaWAN Network-as-a-Service (NaaS) and core network platform.
- **Meshteq role:** Solution design, gateway deployment, sensor integration, analytics platform,
  local project delivery and customer success.
- **Target markets:** Malaysia and selected regional markets (to be expanded based on joint
  commercial agreements).

---

## 2. Partnership Categories

1. **Network Operators / Telcos**
   - Objective: Extend LoRaWAN and IoT coverage, monetise connectivity via vertical solutions.
   - Meshteq contribution: Use-case design, sensors, gateways, dashboards and project delivery.

2. **Technology & Sensor Vendors**
   - Objective: Local implementation, integration and support.
   - Meshteq contribution: Field deployment, firmware integration, cloud platform integration.

3. **Universities & Research Institutions**
   - Objective: Joint R&D on IoT, predictive analytics and infrastructure monitoring.
   - Meshteq contribution: Industry use cases, pilot sites and commercialisation pathways.

4. **System Integrators / Channel Partners**
   - Objective: Bundle Meshteq IoT solutions into broader digital transformation projects.
   - Meshteq contribution: Co-branded or white-label solutions, technical support and training.

---

## 3. Partner Engagement Process

1. Initial discussion and NDA (if required).
2. High-level use-case and market definition.
3. Commercial and technical scoping:
   - Roles and responsibilities
   - Commercial model (project-based, subscription, NaaS, etc.)
4. Pilot or proof of concept.
5. Framework agreement for scaling (country or sector specific).
