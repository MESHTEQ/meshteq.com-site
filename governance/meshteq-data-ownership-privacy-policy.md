# Meshteq Data Ownership & Privacy Policy
Version: 1.0  
Effective Date: March 2026

## 1. Introduction

Meshteq Sdn Bhd is committed to protecting the confidentiality and privacy of data processed through its platforms.

This policy explains how data is collected, processed, stored, and protected across Meshteq services.

Platforms covered include:

- Meshteq.ai
- PrimeTune.ai
- Imyour.ai

---

## 2. Data Ownership Principles

Customers retain full ownership of all operational data generated through use of the Meshteq platform.

Meshteq acts solely as a data processor responsible for securely storing and processing the data to provide services.

Meshteq does not sell or distribute customer data to third parties.

Meshteq retains ownership of its platform technology and intellectual property.

---

## 3. Types of Data Processed

The platform may process:

Operational Data

- IoT telemetry data
- equipment operational parameters
- maintenance records
- calibration data
- asset inventory information
- system alerts and events

Platform Data

- login activity
- system logs
- platform configuration data

Account Data

- user names
- email addresses
- authentication credentials

---

## 4. Data Processing Purpose

Data is processed only to provide platform services including:

- asset monitoring
- predictive maintenance analytics
- calibration compliance management
- operational alerting
- system administration

---

## 5. Data Confidentiality

Customer data is treated as confidential.

Access to data is restricted to authorized personnel and automated systems necessary for service delivery.

Data will not be disclosed to third parties except when:

- required for platform operation
- authorized by the customer
- required by law

---

## 6. Data Security Measures

Meshteq protects data through technical and organizational controls including:

- encrypted data transmission
- role-based access control
- tenant data isolation
- secure cloud infrastructure
- system monitoring and logging

---

## 7. Data Storage Location

Operational data is stored in Supabase cloud infrastructure located in the Singapore region.

---

## 8. Data Retention

Customer data is retained for the duration of the service agreement.

Customers may request export of their data upon termination of service.

Data may be securely deleted after the termination of service subject to backup retention policies.

---

## 9. Aggregated Data Use

Meshteq may use anonymized aggregated data to improve platform functionality and analytics capabilities.

Aggregated data does not identify individual customers.

---

## 10. Customer Responsibilities

Customers are responsible for protecting account credentials and managing user access within their tenant environment.

Customers should notify Meshteq if unauthorized access is suspected.

---

## 11. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated periodically to reflect improvements in platform services and regulatory requirements.

---

## 12. Contact

Questions regarding this policy may be directed to Meshteq Sdn Bhd through official company communication channels.
